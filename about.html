Discover our rich heritage, visionary leadership, and commitment to academic excellence

Our History

Welcome to Mind Power University, where tradition meets innovation in the heart of nature near Nainital, India. Established by the Government of Uttarakhand, MPU offers globally-relevant, skill-based education across Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, Law, and more. Our modern, eco-friendly campus fosters academic excellence, cultural diversity, and holistic growth.

Rooted in 5000 years of rich Indian heritage, we nurture ethical leaders with global mindsets. With world-class faculty, cutting-edge infrastructure, and a curriculum blending life skills and critical thinking, MPU is more than a university

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a globally recognized center of excellence in education that nurtures creative thinkers, ethical leaders, and lifelong learners who will drive positive change in society.

Mission: To provide transformative education through innovative pedagogy, cutting-edge research, and strong industry-academia collaboration that prepares students to meet global challenges and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Pursuing the highest standards in all aspects of education
  • Integrity: Upholding ethical principles and academic honesty
  • Innovation: Fostering creativity and embracing new ideas
  • Inclusivity: Creating an environment that celebrates diversity
  • Social Responsibility: Committing to the betterment of society
