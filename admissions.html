Admission Process Flow

1

Submit Your Query

Reach out to us with your interest in a program. Our team will guide you with the next steps.

  • Submit inquiry form online or via email
  • Mention your preferred course and country of origin
2

Get Personalized Counseling

Our expert counselors will connect with you to understand your goals and suggest suitable programs.

  • One-on-one session (online or phone)
  • Discuss course structure, fees, scholarships, and visa support
3

Fill Application Form (Online)

Complete our application form with all academic and personal details.

  • Fill the online application form
  • Upload required documents
  • Pay application fee
4

Assessment Test / Personal Interview

Appear for the program-specific assessment or interview (if required).

  • Conducted online
  • Evaluates eligibility and interest
  • Next steps for enrollment
5

Offer Letter & Visa Process

Receive your official offer letter once selected. Begin your visa application process.

  • Acceptance letter issued
  • Visa guidance and support provided
6

Reserve Your Seat

Secure your admission by confirming acceptance and submitting the initial fee.

  • Submit fee as per guidelines
  • Get onboarding and welcome details

Admission Requirements

What you'll need to apply to our programs

Academic Records

Official transcripts from all previously attended institutions with minimum GPA requirements.

Identification

Government-issued ID, passport for international students, and proof of age.

Test Scores

Standardized test scores (if required for your program) or English proficiency tests.

Application Fee

Non-refundable application fee payment receipt (waivers available for eligible students).

Personal Statement

Essay or statement of purpose outlining your academic goals and interests.

Recommendations

Letters of recommendation from teachers or professional references.
